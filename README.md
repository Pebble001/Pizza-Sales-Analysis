# Pizza Sales Analysis

A SQL and Power BI project analysing pizza sales data to identify peak trading hours, top-performing products, and revenue trends — helping a pizza business optimise its menu and operations.

---

## Overview

This project digs into a pizza restaurant's transaction data to answer practical business questions: when are customers most likely to order, what do they buy, and which products are underperforming?

**Business Questions:**
- What days and times generate the most orders?
- Which pizza categories and sizes drive the most revenue?
- Which pizzas are bestsellers and which should be reviewed?

---

## Tools Used
- **SQL** — data extraction and aggregation
- **Power BI** — dashboard and visualisations
- **Excel** — source data

---

## Key Findings

**Sales Timing**
- Orders peak on **weekends — especially Fridays and Saturdays**
- Busiest hours: **12pm–1pm** (lunch) and **5pm–8pm** (dinner)

**Category & Size Performance**
- **Classic** pizza category drives the highest number of orders
- **Large size** pizzas contribute the most revenue

**Top & Bottom Performers**
| | Pizza |
|---|---|
| Best sellers | Classic Deluxe, Barbecue Chicken |
| Lowest seller | Brie Carre |

## Recommendations

2. Consider promotional offers at 12pm to boost lunchtime orders
